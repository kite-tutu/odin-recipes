Cheesecake Recipe

About Lasagna Recipe

These lemon cheesecake creme brulee croissants are filled with a mixture of lemon curd and no-bake lemon cheesecake filling, then glazed with caramelized sugar syrup for that creme brulee crunch.

Ingredients

  • 6 large croissants
  • 1 cup cheesecake filling, such as PhiladelphiaÂ® Cheesecake No-Bake
  • 1/4 cup lemon curd
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup water

Steps

  1. Combine cheesecake filling, lemon curd and lemon zest in a bowl; stir well.
  2. Use a paring knife to make a small cut into the side of each croissant.
  3. Add sugar and water to a small saucepan and bring to a simmer over medium-high heat.
